## Features

- **Centralized dashboard** consolidating multiple Twilio accounts into a single, secure interface.
- **Bulk lead management and engagement** allowing users to upload CSV files containing hundreds of leads at once and then creating message templates with editable variety to reach out to those leads all at once.
- **Secure authentication system** ensuring only authorized staff can access or send communications.
- **Data storage and management** optimized for handling large lead datasets efficiently.
- **Automated lead handoff** — positively engaged leads are routed directly to the sales department.
- **Responsive design** ensuring full functionality across an array of devices.
